How it Works

Self-testing will cause the system to present you with a question from the 2019 viva release for the subjects you've selected, giving you a reading time and viva time countdown for you to practice your spiels.

Working in a study group will bring up the title of a past question and a timer, so that the mock examiner can see the laptop screen and find the relevant past question from the common database shared amongst sixth years.
